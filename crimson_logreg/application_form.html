IDENTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

The following requirements must be uploaded:

  • Birth certificate of both applicants
  • Certificate of No Marriage(CENOMAR from NSO) of applicants
  • Family Planning Certificate- From the population Office(as schedule)
  • Consent of Parents-if applicant is 18 years but below 21 yrs of age
  • Advice of Parents-if applicant is 21 years but below 25 years of age
  • Certificate of Legal Capacity to marry-if applicant is foreigner
  • Photocopy of Passport (arrival stamp)
